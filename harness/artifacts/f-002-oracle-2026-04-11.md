## Interpretation

Deltas are small but systematic — max ≈ 0.026 RMB / 0.057pp, concentrated in high-margin SKUs. Root cause: workbook `毛利率` column is stored with ~2 significant decimal places (e.g., 65.8000%), while `定价`/`成本` have higher precision. This is a **data-precision/rounding issue in the workbook**, not a formula error. Two remediation paths:

1. **Relax threshold to 0.001 (0.1pp / 0.05 RMB)** — passes all rows; appropriate given real-world rounding
2. **Fix workbook source** — round 定价/成本 to match the precision of 毛利率
